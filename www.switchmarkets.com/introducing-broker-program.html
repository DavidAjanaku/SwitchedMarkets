FAQs

How often do I get paid?

Monthly. Current month commissions generated by your referrals will be summarized at the beginning of next month and sent to you by Wire Transfer. Steady revenue stream by your clients' trading activity. Earn as your customers trade!

What marketing materials do you provide?

Anything you want, tell us! Customized banners, landing pages, copy, you just tell us what you need and we’ll create it for you.

What marketing channels would you suggest?

Social Media is the main driver nowadays. Facebook Ads, Google Ads, YouTube Ads, Facebook Groups and Instagram may work efficiently for you. But if you struggle to find where to start on your own, and need to get more tips for your particular situation, get in touch with us, and we will assist you!

What marketing methods am I not allowed to do?

You are not allowed to do the following:

  • -Send Spam Emails
  • -Spamming people on social media, blogs, forums, or any other community platform
  • -Any illegal or deceptive type of paid marketing such as bidding on our brand keywords such as "Switch Markets" and "SwitchMarkets.com"

If we find that an IB is trying to circumvent these rules, commissions will be voided and no payments made.
