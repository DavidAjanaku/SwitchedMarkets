How to Download and Install an EA

Choose and download the EA by clicking the ‘Download’ button above.

Copy and paste the file into the Experts folder on your computer. This is normally located in the C: drive.

Launch the MetaTrader (MT4) platform.

Look for the Expert Advisors section positioned underneath the Navigator panel on the left side of the MT4 platform.

Click on the Plus symbol and all the available EAs will appear.

Click on the EA you want to install, drag it onto one of the charts on your platform and watch the magic happen.
