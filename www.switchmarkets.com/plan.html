FAQs

Read our frequently asked questions below. If you still need help, contact us today.

How do I get started?

We're glad you’re ready to get started with us. Just click "Start trading today" and you'll start setting up your account. Have a look at our two account types; Pro and Standard and see which suits your trading needs better. Once you've decided, it takes a few minutes to go through our form, then you’ll be ready to trade once approved!

How long does the demo account last for?

A demo account with Switch Markets is accessible forever, there is no limit as long as you trade. Then, when you’re ready you can open a live trading account by clicking "Start trading today" at any time. Please be aware that inactive demo accounts are deactivated after 45 days of inactivity.

What is the minimum amount needed to open a Switch Markets trading account?

With Switch Markets you can open a live trading account with as little as $50.

What costs are involved in trading with Switch Markets?

That's an easy one. There are zero deposit fees and small withdrawal fees. Pick a trading account that works for your trading type, either Pro or Standard.

Do I get charged for a dormant trading account?

Nope. Nada. You will not be charged anything for keeping your account open.

How fast are withdrawals?

Fast. When a withdrawal is requested and assuming you’ve passed all your routine verification checks, we’ll have your funds with you. Most of the withdrawals are completed within one business day.

How do I contact support?

We’d love to chat. First up, speak to us on our live chat. We’ve got a team of real humans on standby 24/5 via our live chat. You can also contact us via email at
