How does the lot size calculator work?

In just a few simple steps, you can calculate the correct lot size for your trade depending on your desired risk.

  • Select the instrument you’re trading and the base currency of your Forex trading account
  • Fill in the entry-level and the stop loss for your planned trade
  • Fill in the size of your account
  • Select your desired risk (either % of your account balance or a fixed amount)
  • Click “Calculate” and let our lot size calculator do the hard work for you

Forex Calculator Glossary

Instrument

Also referred to as "Symbol". The currency pair you're choosing to trade from the major forex crosses

Deposit currency

The currency in which your account balance is nominated (account currency or account base currency)

Entry level

The price at which the trade is or will be opened on the Forex market

Stop-loss level

The price at which trade closes in the negative scenario

Take profit level

The price at which trade closes in the positive scenario

Risk Tolerance

Type of risk ratio you want to calculate with. Can be either "percentage" or "fixed amount"
