What is an Islamic Trading Account?

Islamic Trading Account, also known as 'No Swap Account' is a specific type of trading account that follows the Sharia laws and therefore allows Muslims to engage in trading activities.

Unlike conventional finance, finance in Islamic countries is mainly driven by ethical goals and considerations. As such, it comes with different rules and regulations.

These are all considered in our Swap free accounts and therefore Muslim traders do NOT pay swap, interest or overnight charges on ANY trades for Forex, Stocks, Metals & Indices.

However, if a trader holds a position open for over five days, a flat rate fee will be charged. Please see the fixed rates below.
